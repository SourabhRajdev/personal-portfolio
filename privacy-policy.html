1. Introduction

Welcome to SourabhRajdev.space ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

Information You Provide

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as:

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Types of Cookies We Use

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website performance:

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Our website may contain third-party cookies from services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security measures include:

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
